Official Biography of St. Carlo Acutis

St. Carlo Acutis

Carlo Acutis was born on May 3, 1991, in London, to Andrea Acutis and Antonia Salzano. From a young age, Carlo displayed a profound love for God and the Church that inspired his parents. His family later moved to Milan, Italy, where Carlo’s passion for computer programming blossomed alongside his deepening faith. His feast day is celebrated on October 12.

Canonization

St. Carlo Acutis was canonized on September 7, 2025, becoming the first millennial saint. Known for his devotion to the Eucharist and skill with technology, Carlo created a website documenting Eucharistic miracles worldwide. His beatification took place in 2020 after the recognition of his first miracle, and a second miracle led to his canonization.

Devotion & Contributions

At the age of seven, Carlo began attending daily Mass and praying the Rosary. He is remembered for his words: “The Eucharist is my highway to Heaven”. Using his programming skills, he cataloged 187 Eucharistic miracles across the world, making him known as the “Patron Saint of the Internet.”

Miracles

  • The Healing of Matheus Vianna (Brazil): A boy suffering from a rare pancreatic disorder was inexplicably healed through Carlo’s intercession.
  • The Healing of Valeria Valverde (Costa Rica): After severe head trauma in a bicycle accident, Valeria miraculously recovered following her mother’s prayer at Carlo’s tomb in Assisi.

Legacy

Pope Francis praised Carlo for showing young people that “true happiness is found by putting God first and serving him in our brothers.” His life is a witness that holiness is possible in the modern digital world, inspiring countless young Catholics.

Prayer to St. Carlo Acutis

Dear Father,
Who has given us the ardent testimony of the young Servant of God, Carlo Acutis, who made the Eucharist the core of his life and the strength of his daily commitments so that everybody may love You above all else...

Confirm my Faith,
nurture my Hope,
strengthen my Charity,
in the image of young Carlo,
who, growing in these virtues,
now lives with You.

Grant me the grace I really need....

I trust in You, Father, and your Beloved Son Jesus, in the Virgin Mary, our Dearest Mother, and in the intervention of Your Servant, Carlo Acutis.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be

Official Prayer & Novena to St. Carlo Acutis

Opening Prayer:
Most Holy Trinity, Father, Son and Holy Spirit, I thank you for all the grace and favours with which you enriched the soul of the Servant of God Carlo Acutis during his 15 years on this earth. Through the merits of this Angel of Youth, grant me the grace which I earnestly seek... (ask the grace which you seek)

First Day Meditation: "Not me but God"

St. Carlo, you who made your life a continual renunciation and setting aside of unimportant things, grant me the grace to overcome all things and desires that are transient. Amen.

Second Day Meditation: "Always to be united with Jesus, that is my life's programme."

St. Carlo, you who lived in the heart of Jesus, give me the grace to apply God's plan of love to everything. Amen.

Third Day Meditation: "Continuously ask your Guardian Angel for help."

St. Carlo, you who saw the certainty of your Angel's presence, give me the Grace to live righteously, as my Guardian Angel desires. Amen.

Fourth Day Meditation: "Confession is like the fire enabling the soul to rise again."

St. Carlo, you who experienced God’s mercy, give me the grace to constantly seek confession and deep contrition. Amen.

Fifth Day Meditation: "Sadness is looking at ourselves, happiness is looking towards God."

St. Carlo, you who always looked towards Jesus, give me the grace to live genuine joy. Amen.

Sixth Day Meditation: "The only thing we have to ask God for, is the desire to be holy."

St. Carlo, give me the grace of a deep desire for Heaven. Amen.

Seventh Day Meditation: "The Virgin Mary is the only Woman in my life."

St. Carlo, you who loved the Virgin Mary above all women, give me the grace to respond to her love and example. Amen.

Eighth Day Meditation: "The Eucharist is my highway to Heaven."

St. Carlo, you who sought Jesus in the Tabernacle, give me the grace of great devotion to the Blessed Sacrament. Amen.

Ninth Day Meditation: "I am happy to die because I have lived my life without wasting a minute on things displeasing to God."

St. Carlo, give me perseverance to the end and a holy death. Amen.

Final Prayer:
Almighty God, Father of Mercy, raise your Servant Carlo Acutis to the glory of the altars, so that through him you will be even more glorified. Give us the honour of calling him Blessed and Saint in your Church. And through his merit, give me the grace that I so ardently desire. Amen.

Each day, pray five "Our Fathers," five "Hail Marys," and five "Glory Be to the Father," in thanksgiving for the graces granted to Carlo.
