Our Educational Institutions

St. Aloysius High School, Manalumkal

St. Aloysius High School is a reputed aided secondary institution in Manalumkal, Kottayam. It stands as a pillar of education and faith, offering quality learning and holistic development for students in the Akalakunnam region.

Holy Family LPS Manalumkal

Established in 1916, Holy Family Lower Primary School serves as a cornerstone for early education in the community. The school continues to uphold values of excellence and compassion in the spirit of Christian education.
